What Happens After Registration?

  1. 1
    Document Review

    The SUG Welfare team reviews your submitted documents within 24-48 hours.

  2. 2
    Record Cross-Check

    Your matriculation number is verified against the university's official records.

  3. 3
    Identity Verification

    Your NIN and passport photo are cross-referenced with campus security databases.

  4. 4
    Approval & Agent ID

    Upon successful verification, you receive a unique Agent ID and dashboard access via email and SMS.

  5. 5
    Start Listing

    You can now create property listings, manage inquiries, and connect with students.

Requirements

  • Valid FUNAAB matriculation number
  • National Identification Number (NIN)
  • Passport-sized photograph
  • Recommendation from a caretaker
  • Active phone number
