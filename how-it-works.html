How funaabhub Works

A simple, transparent process to connect students with verified housing agents at FUNAAB.

1

Register

Fill out the agent registration form with your matric number, NIN, department, and area of coverage. Upload a valid ID and a passport photograph.

2

Verify

The SUG Welfare Directorate reviews your submitted documents, cross-checks your matriculation records, and verifies your identity with the campus security unit.

3

Get Listed

Once approved, you receive a unique Agent ID and access to your dashboard. Start creating listings, managing bookings, and connecting with students.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can register as an agent on funaabhub?

Any student of FUNAAB who has a valid matriculation number, a National Identification Number (NIN), and is recommended by a recognized caretaker or property owner can register. The SUG Welfare Directorate must verify all documents before approval.

How long does verification take?

Verification typically takes 24 to 48 hours. You will receive an email and SMS notification once your account is approved. If there are any issues with your documents, the team will reach out for clarification.

What areas are covered on the platform?

funaabhub covers all major student housing areas around FUNAAB including Camp, Harmony, Gateway, Aluta, and Campus Extension. Agents are verified for specific areas based on where they operate.

Is there a fee to register?

No. Registration on funaabhub is completely free for both agents and students. The platform is funded and managed by the SUG Welfare Directorate as part of its mandate to protect student interests.

What happens if I encounter a scam agent?

Use the "Report Scam" page to submit a report with all relevant details. The SUG will investigate the complaint, and if the agent is found guilty, their verification will be revoked and they will be blacklisted from the platform.

Can students also create listings?

Students can browse and contact verified agents, but only registered and verified agents can create property listings on the platform. This ensures all listings are traceable and accountable.

How do I verify an agent before paying?

Go to the "Verify or Report an Agent" page and enter the agent's name or Agent ID. The system will show their verification status, area of coverage, and any reports filed against them. Always verify before making payments.
