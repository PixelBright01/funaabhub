About the Initiative

funaabhub was created by the SUG Welfare Directorate to bring accountability to the informal and often exploitative housing market around the student community.

Today, students find agents through word-of-mouth or unverified flyers, with no way to confirm who an agent is, whether they're legitimate, or whether they've been reported for scams. This platform replaces that with a registry the SUG welfare office controls and vets.

"Every student deserves to find housing without fear of scams or extortion. This platform is our commitment to making that a reality." — SUG Welfare Director

How Verification Works

A simple 3-step process to become a verified agent on funaabhub.

1

Register

Fill out the registration form with your matric number, department, NIN/ID, area of coverage, and caretaker details.

2

Verify

SUG Welfare office reviews your documents, cross-checks your matric number and NIN against institutional records.

3

Get Listed

Once approved, you receive a unique Agent ID, a verification badge, and can start creating house listings.

Register as an Agent

Submit your details for SUG verification. Approval typically takes 24-48 hours.
