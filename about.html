About funaabhub

The SUG-backed platform built to protect students from housing scams and connect them with verified agents.

Our Mission

To create a safe, transparent, and trustworthy student housing ecosystem at FUNAAB by verifying agents, standardizing listings, and providing students with the information they need to make safe housing decisions.

Our Vision

To become the gold standard for student housing verification across all Nigerian universities, eliminating rental fraud and ensuring every student can find housing with confidence.

A SUG Welfare Initiative

funaabhub was born out of a pressing need identified by the FUNAAB Students' Union Government (SUG) Welfare Directorate. Every session, dozens of students fall victim to housing scams, paying large sums to unverified agents who disappear after collecting money.

In response, the SUG launched funaabhub as a centralized, verified directory of trusted housing agents. Every agent on this platform has been personally vetted by the welfare team, with their matriculation records cross-checked against university databases and their identity documents verified.

The platform is not a business venture. It is a public good, funded by the SUG, for the benefit of every FUNAAB student. Our goal is simple: no student should lose their money to a scam agent when there is a system designed to protect them.

Milestones

January 2025

Platform Conceptualized

The SUG Welfare Directorate identified housing scams as the top student welfare issue and began designing a verification solution.

April 2025

Prototype Launched

A pilot version was released to 30 pre-vetted agents. Feedback from students and agents shaped the full platform design.

September 2025

Official Launch

funaabhub went live with 120 verified agents covering Camp, Harmony, Gateway, Aluta, and Campus Extension areas.

January 2026

500 Students Connected

Over 500 students used the platform to find verified agents and safe housing, with zero scam reports from registered agents.

June 2026

Expansion Planning

The SUG is in talks with other university SUGs to replicate the funaabhub model across Nigeria.
